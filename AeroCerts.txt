Get Certifications like Cat A, B1, B2 then C.

Category A : Certifying technician (basic technician)
Category B1: Line maintenance certifying engineer - mechanical (manager for mechanical components and systems)
Category B2: Line maintenance certifying engineer - avionic    (manager for electrical components and systems)
Category C : Base maintenance certifying engineer              (manager for entire aircraft, signs for complete maintenance)
